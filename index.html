Crowdsourcing services

According to Wikipedia crowdsourcing is outsourcing some tasks to the crowds - a bunch of people living and working in different parts of the world. There are currently two major players in the field of crowdsourcing services: one is Amazon Mechanical Turk and the other one is CrowdFlower.

CrowdFlowerClient is a simple client in Java using CrowdFlower RESTful web service API. With it you can:

  • create new job without data
  • create new job by uploading data in JSON or CSV
  • upload data to existing job

It is used in one of my other projects -- an extension for OpenRefine (@OpenRefine) (previously known as Google Refine). Check out other projects at my profile page (see @sparkica).
